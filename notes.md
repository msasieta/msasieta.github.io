**[Interior Microstates and Black Hole Entropy](/assets/review_BHentropy.pdf)**

Semiclassical gravity admits a vast set of candidate black hole interior states, raising the question of which of these correspond to independent quantum microstates that account for black hole entropy. In this review, we survey several explicit constructions of black hole interior microstates in AdS$_2$ holography and AdS/CFT and assess whether they furnish bases of the black hole Hilbert space. We further highlight the settings in which non-perturbative effects in the gravitational path integral, captured by spacetime wormholes, resolve the resulting overcounting and reproduce the black hole entropy from state counting.
